# Preferred Merged Continuation

This folder contains the preferred merged zero-draft continuation of the Lars Odin novel from Chapter 21 through Chapter 55. It starts immediately after Chapter 20, where Mira Sovan agrees to current evidence, not memory work, while hostile ships approach Aphex Dependency L-Prime 41.

## Premise

Version C's KBC Void, Creator, Nomad, and Lyra-recovery mystery is the structural spine. The merge sharpens the pressure line from Version A: Black Hole B-7 is actively building biological acquisition capacity, Fastulv is used as the visible wolf, and Havets Sista Sång remains the hidden sea until exposure becomes a consented cost. Version B contributes the inhabited social fabric: Fabron's Clear Hand order, Vigor's kitchens and logistics, NUKEA repair culture, station maintenance guilds, and hidden Aerits whose consent and refusal remain real.

The outcome preserves Mira's personhood, prevents Lars from becoming central command, reduces Black Hole B-7's immediate mass-acquisition capacity, blocks Aphex and NUKEA from owning the recovery apparatus, and leaves the KBC Void/Nomad/Lyra mystery open as hard-sci-fi evidence rather than magic.
