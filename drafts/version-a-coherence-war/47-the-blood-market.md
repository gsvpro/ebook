# Chapter 47: The Blood Market

The blood market moved inside a refrigerated convoy disguised as vaccine relief. Vigor found the food discrepancy. Mira found the cryo-stress pattern. Fabron found the escort corruption. Lars found the part where everyone expected him to be subtle and ruined it by docking Fastulv nose-first into the convoy's command barge. The fight spilled through cold corridors where stolen tissue samples hung in coded cylinders and Black Hole med-techs wore armor grown from failed experiments. Grim moved like a silver nightmare, breaking weapons and apologizing to one door he had to remove from its hinges. Fabron's Aphex soldiers fought under strict evidence protocols because dead enemies were easier than prosecuted networks but less useful. Mira located the live prisoners by listening to coolant vibration; some were hidden people, most were ordinary victims whose biology had been useful for calibration. Lars reached the central freezer as the market master initiated thermal purge. Havets Sista Sång folded a heat sink through three centimeters of impossible geometry and stole the purge into nowhere local. Every cylinder survived. The market master did not. He tried to inject himself with a stolen Aerit-derived serum and dissolved into black scaffolding before Lars could stop him. The evidence went to twenty-seven jurisdictions at once. Black Hole lost money, samples, and secrecy. It did not lose appetite.
