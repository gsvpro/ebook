# Chapter 53: Coherence Breaks

Victory cost them Sera. The guardian who had wanted broader activation took a strike team into a Black Hole relay embedded near the Creator door and held the channel open long enough for Havets to poison the harvest geometry. She did it with consent, with full model access, and with three separate chances to withdraw. That did not make it clean. Lars heard her last transmission as heat, math, and a laugh that reminded him of people he had failed centuries earlier. 'Coherence breaks,' Sera said, 'so others can remain distributed.' Then her channel vanished. Not dead, Grim cautioned. Not recoverable, Valdrun answered. Mira stood beside Lars during the silence, not touching him because she had learned his grief needed rails more than comfort. Fabron removed his cap. Across the human networks, no one was told a guardian had fallen. They were told a relay crew had held long enough to save three systems, which was also true. Lars wanted the true name spoken. Valdrun said later. Evidence had to move first. Sera's sacrifice collapsed the forced door from harvest net to unstable ring. Black Hole's central fleet, committed too deep, lost synchronization and began to fail like a body rejecting stolen organs.
